About Srinivas Institute of Technology

Srinivas Institute of Technology (SIT) aims towards high standard of education with a holistic approach. The college is fully equipped with state of the art facilities to train budding engineers with an objective to stimulate young minds in the field of technical education. The Institute started in the year 2006 which is a unit of A.Shama Rao Foundation, Mangaluru. The college is located at the Srinivas Campus spread over 15 acres of land at Valachil, Arkula Village, about 10kms from Mangaluru city, towards BC Road, adjacent to NH66. The Institute hosts over 3000 students studying under VTU spanning 10 UG programs, 5 PG programs and 6 Research programs.

About the Conference

The aim of the ICTIR-2023 is to create a platform to foster thinking and innovation in technology and maintain the competence of the profession. Industry 4.0 revolution is evolving exponentially and its extensive domain knowledge will help us leverage solutions in every growing industry today. The conference intends to have deliberations on advanced topics belonging to Electronics and Electrical Engineering with Applications in different Engineering domains in Industry. The theoretical topics together with real-time research from various streams are expected to be presented and discussed in the conference. Deliberations of the conference shall be done but not limited to the following four-track themes. The conference will be conducted in a hybrid mode i.e online and offline mode.

  • Track 1: Artificial Intelligence
  • Track 2:Applied Electronics
  • Track 3: Technology in Management

  • Track 4: Electrical Engineering
